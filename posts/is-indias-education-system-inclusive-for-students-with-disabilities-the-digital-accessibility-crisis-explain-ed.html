Is India’s Education System Inclusive for Students with Disabilities? The Digital Accessibility Crisis Explain ed

Is India’s education system truly inclusive for students with disabilities? No. While India possesses a robust legislative framework via the RPwD Act 2016 and the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020, the system remains structurally integrated but functionally exclusive. Students are being enrolled in record numbers, yet they are met with a "Digital Ceiling"—a barrier of inaccessible software, untagged content, and platform failures that prevent actual learning. Inclusion in India currently exists on paper; on the ground, it is a series of locked digital doors.
